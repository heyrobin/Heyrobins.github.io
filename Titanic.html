Here is my analysis for SARS-CoV-2 a.k.a corona-virus. It is a severe acute respiratory syndrome caused by coronavirus 2. The virus was first identified in December 2019 in the district of China, Wuhan. Later on 30 January 2020, The World Health Organization declared SARS-CoV-2(covid-19) as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern. Right after 1.5 months on 11 March 2020, it was declared as a Pandemic.
